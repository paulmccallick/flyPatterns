# Fly Pattern App

The fly pattern app helps anglers determine what flies to try in the field. It very targeted and leaves much up to the angler. It provids a chart of the life stages of Caddis, MayFly, and StoneFlies. The chart has an image of each lifecycle of the insect, and images and names of the flies that imitate the insect/lifecycle combo. It is intended to be a quick reference when at the fly shop or on the water.

It does not:

- use the camera to try to determine an insect or fly.
- try to match locations or seasons with possible hatches

It does:

- Have accurate photos of representative flies and insects that are static and not generated by AI.
- Have high quality images

# User Focus

The app is targeted at anglers not entemologists. To that end, it should only display life cycles releveant to fishing. If two life cycles have identical flies they should be collapsed.

The user will need to be told whether the pattern is meant to be fished wet, dry, or streamer.

# App usage

This application is a single page with links at the top to the 3 insects. Each section simply has an image of a stage of life next to images of the patterns that imitate it. Under the imitation patterns is the name of the pattern. It is formatted to look great on mobile as that's primarily how it will be used.
